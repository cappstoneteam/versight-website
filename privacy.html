Privacy Policy — Versight

Last updated: May 5, 2026  ·  Developer: John Keller  ·  cappstoneteam@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Versight (“Versight”, “we”, “us”, “our”), operated by John Keller, collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and associated AI-powered features (collectively, the “Services”).

Key points at a glance:
  • We collect information you provide (name, email) and data generated automatically by your use of the Services.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account information such as your name and email address.
  • Content and messages you input when using the Services, including conversations with AI features.
  • Communications you send to us, including support requests, surveys, and feedback.
  • Payment-related information processed through Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or Stripe. Full payment card details are processed by those platforms and are not stored by Versight.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device data: operating system, device type and model, browser type, screen resolution, IP address, unique identifiers, language settings, and general geographic location.
  • Usage data: pages or screens viewed, time spent, navigation paths, access times, and engagement with our communications.

We may use cookies, local storage technologies, and web beacons (pixel tags) to collect this data automatically.

Sensitive Information

In the course of using the Services, you may share sensitive information (e.g., religious beliefs, health topics). By providing such information, you consent to its use as described in this Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide the Services. To create and manage your account, process transactions, and deliver the features of the Services.

To communicate with you. To send service-related announcements, reminders, security alerts, and administrative messages.

To improve and protect the Services. To personalize your experience, perform troubleshooting and research, and detect and prevent fraudulent or harmful activity.

For research and development. We may analyze usage and conversation data in aggregate or de-identified form to improve the Services and train AI models.

For legal compliance. To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal process, and protect the rights and safety of Versight, users, and others.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for interest-based advertising.

Service providers. We may engage third-party vendors for cloud hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment processing. These parties access information only as needed to perform services on our behalf.

Business transfers. If Versight undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Legal disclosures. We may disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect our rights, the safety of users, or to investigate fraud.

4. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

  • Access. Request information about what personal data we hold and how we use it.
  • Correction. Request correction of inaccurate or outdated personal information.
  • Deletion. Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Withdrawal of consent. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Opt-out. Unsubscribe from promotional communications via the unsubscribe link in emails or by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cappstoneteam@gmail.com. You may also delete your account through the app settings.

5. California Residents — CCPA / CPRA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know. Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, sources, purposes, and third parties with whom it is shared.
  • Right to Delete. Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct. Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing. We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To submit a CCPA/CPRA request, contact us at cappstoneteam@gmail.com.

6. Children

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us immediately at cappstoneteam@gmail.com and we will delete that information and revoke access.

9. Third-Party Services
